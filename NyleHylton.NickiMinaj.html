Who is she?

Onika Tanya Maraj-Petty (born December 8, 1982), known professionally as Nicki Minaj, is a Trinidadian-born[a]
rapper and singer based in the United States. Often referred to as the "Queen of Rap", she is known for her
musical versatility, animated rap flow, and alter egos. She first gained recognition after releasing three
mixtapes between 2007 and 2009.
